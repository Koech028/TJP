FAQs

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login screen, click "Forgot password", enter your email, and follow the link sent to you. You'll be able to set a new password securely.
How do I add a new trade?
Navigate to the Dashboard, click the "New Trade" button, fill out the trade information including entry/exit prices, position size, and notes, then click "Save".
Can I import data from other trading journals?
Yes! We support CSV imports from most trading platforms. Go to Settings → Import Data to get started. Our system will guide you through the mapping process.
How do I generate performance reports?
Navigate to the Reports section, select the date range and metrics you want to include, then click "Generate Report". You can export reports as PDF or CSV.
Is my trading data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your trading information is always private and secure.
